Welcome to Wordly

Game Rules

- The goal of the game is to guess the word chosen by the computer.

- You get three chances to take a guess at the word. If you guess within 3 attempts, you win.

- You will also have 2 hints that you can use for assistance.

- Reset the game if you wish to play again.

- If you give up midway, you can use the "Show Word" button.
